Semantics in HTML

Semantics meaning - "the study of the meanings of words and phrases"-Google Semantics makes the content of the webpage more descriptive so that it becomes helpful for screen readers and thus it makes the site more accessible for people with desparities.

Articles are the independent titles which may/may not be related to with the actual content of the website.

Section tags are those which have direct relation to the main content of the website and plays an integral part in the navigation of the site.

Aside tag

HTML element represents a portion of a document whose content is only indirectly related to the document's main content. Asides are frequently presented as sidebars or call-out boxes

Footer is the last ending part of every website which contains details like © and legal details etc,.
