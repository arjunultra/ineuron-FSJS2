Block Vs Inline

Block Tags

Block level elements are those which takes up the entire line width of their container irrespective of their content.Eg: heading tags,paragraph tags,div tag,etc,.


Inline Tags

Inline tags are the opposite of block tags where they only takeup the space needed as per the content present within and can share the space with other in-line elements.Eg: 'a'tag,image tag,span,etc,.
